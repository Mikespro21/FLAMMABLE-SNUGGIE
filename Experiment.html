The Experiment

Goal: Measure how well different materials (and combinations) keep heat in a cup of water over 10 minutes.
🥄
Fill cup with hot water
🧃
Wrap with one material
📏
Record temperature at start
⏳
Wait 10 minutes
🌡️
Record final temperature
🔥
Repeat with most layers (Snuggie!):
  • Cotton
  • Cardboard
  • Paper
  • Plastic
  • We didnt use foil due to the poor insulating evidence
📊
What we measured: Initial/final temperatures, change from Initial to Final and heat loss for each setup, three trials each!
